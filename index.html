The Artist

The King Of Desi Hip-Hop

Yo Yo Honey Singh is not just a musician — he is a cultural phenomenon. Born Hirdesh Singh, he redefined Punjabi music and became one of India's highest-paid music artists, bridging the worlds of hip-hop, pop, and Bollywood.

From chart-busting hits like Brown Rang, Lungi Dance, Dheere Dheere, and Urvashi to stadium-filling anthems — Honey Singh's music has been the soundtrack of a generation.

His comeback and continued dominance in the music industry cements his status as an irreplaceable icon. Watch him own the JLN Stadium stage like never before.

Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

After completing your purchase, you will receive an e-ticket instantly via the email address you provided. The ticket contains a unique QR code that is scanned at the venue entrance. Please ensure you carry either a printed copy or have it on your phone.
All ticket sales are final and non-refundable. However, in the event of show cancellation or postponement by the organizer, full refunds or exchange options will be offered within 7–10 business days. Ticket transfers to another person are permitted.
You must carry a valid government-issued photo ID matching the name on your ticket — such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Driving Licence, or Voter ID. Children under 5 years of age enter free of charge when accompanied by a paying adult.
Doors open at 6:00 PM and the main show begins at 8:00 PM. We strongly recommend arriving at least 1–1.5 hours before the show to clear security and find your section. VIP and Gold Zone ticket holders will have access to dedicated fast-track entry gates.
Personal mobile phones are permitted for photos and short video clips. Professional cameras, DSLRs, external lenses, tripods, and dedicated video recorders are strictly prohibited unless you hold a verified press credential. Drones are not permitted.
Prohibited items include: outside food and beverages, professional cameras/video equipment, weapons of any kind, fireworks, glass bottles, narcotics, and oversized bags. All items are subject to security screening at entry. The venue staff reserves the right to deny entry.
Take the Delhi Metro Violet Line to JLN Stadium Metro Station. The stadium is a 2-minute walk from Gate 1 of the station. Additional shuttle services will be available from Hazrat Nizamuddin Station. Parking is available within the stadium complex on a first-come basis.
