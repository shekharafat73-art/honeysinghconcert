The Artist

The King Of Desi Hip-Hop

Yo Yo Honey Singh is not just a musician — he is a cultural phenomenon. Born Hirdesh Singh, he redefined Punjabi music and became one of India's highest-paid music artists, bridging the worlds of hip-hop, pop, and Bollywood.

My Story — India Chapter is an honest, high-energy journey through Honey Singh's rise, fall & comeback. From silence to stadiums, YoYo presents his story where every fan wants to know — his rise, his roar & his resilience.

Come and be a part of this live musical documentary sung by the man himself. His biggest hits from all eras, a powerful mix of music, memories, emotions and swagger.
